## Sourcing notes

- **Press photos:** [the-avenues.com](https://www.the-avenues.com/), Mabanee press releases, [Gensler project page](https://www.gensler.com/projects/the-avenues)
- **Hero / district reels:** The Avenues YouTube channel — clip 10-20 second segments; encode 1920×1080 h264 MP4 at ~5 Mbps + optional AV1/WebM for size
- **AI assets:** Generate with Midjourney v7 / Imagen 4 / similar; label every AI image with the `⌬ AI-generated` glyph

## Compliance

- No AI-generated real people. No fake celebrity appearances.
- No AI-generated brand logos or trade dress.
- Tenant logos used only in editorial context, no endorsement implied (note in UI).
- If any rights-holder objects to an asset, please contact the maintainer and it will be removed within 24 hours.
